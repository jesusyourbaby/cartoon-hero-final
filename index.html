The Amazing Spiderman

About:

Spider-Man is the superhero alter ego of Peter Parker, a high school student from New York City who gains spider-like powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses his abilities to fight crime and protect the city while facing personal struggles and responsibilities. Created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, Spider-Man.
